### 1. Introduction

Welcome to Secure Values ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

**Key Principle**: We believe in privacy by design. Our App is built to collect, store, and transmit as little data as possible.

---

### 2. Information We Collect

#### 2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:**
- Personal identification information
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports
- Advertising IDs
- Third-party tracking data

#### 2.2 Information Stored Locally (On Your Device Only)

The App stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- **User Account Information**: Username and encrypted password hash (stored locally)
- **Personal Values**: The 5 values you choose to store
- **Victory Logs**: Your victory entries and notes
- **Detection Logs**: Local detection history
- **App Preferences**: Settings you configure

**All locally stored data is encrypted** using AES-256 encryption.

#### 2.3 Data Transmission

**We do NOT transmit any data externally:**
- No data is sent to our servers
- No data is sent to third-party services
- No analytics are collected
- No telemetry is transmitted
- All processing happens on your device

---

### 3. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect or transmit data externally, there is no external use of your information.

**Local Use Only:**
- Store your personal values (on your device)
- Maintain your victory log (on your device)
- Provide app functionality (on your device)
- Remember your preferences (on your device)

---

### 4. Data Storage and Security

#### 4.1 Local Storage

All data is stored **exclusively on your device** in an encrypted database:
- **Encryption**: AES-256 encryption via SQLCipher
- **Password Hashing**: bcrypt with cost factor 12
- **Secure Storage**: Hardware-backed secure storage when available
- **No Cloud Sync**: Data never leaves your device

#### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted database storage
- Secure password hashing
- Hardware-backed security (when available)
- No external network transmission
- No third-party service integration

#### 4.3 Your Responsibility

- You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device
- We recommend using device lock screen protection
- Enable biometric authentication if available
- Keep your device software updated

---

### 5. Data Sharing

**We do NOT share your data with anyone:**
- No third-party services
- No advertisers
- No analytics providers
- No cloud storage services
- No social media platforms
- No data brokers

**Your data stays on your device. Period.**

### 6. Third-Party Services

Our App does **NOT** integrate with third-party services that would collect your data:
- No analytics services
- No advertising networks
- No cloud storage providers
- No social media APIs
- No crash reporting services

**Exception**: We use open-source libraries for core functionality (encryption, database). These libraries operate locally and do not transmit data.

### 7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us, and we will delete it.

---

### 8. Your Rights and Choices

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- **Access**: All your data is accessible within the App
- **Deletion**: You can delete your account or data through the App settings
- **Export**: You can export your data through the App's backup feature
- **Control**: You have complete control over your data

**To delete all data:**
1. Open the App
2. Go to Settings
3. Select "Delete Account" or "Clear All Data"
4. Confirm deletion

**Note**: Deleting the App will also delete all locally stored data.

---

### 9. Data Retention

We do not retain data externally because we do not collect it.

**Locally Stored Data:**
- Retained as long as you use the App
- Deleted when you delete your account
- Deleted when you uninstall the App
- You can manually delete data at any time
