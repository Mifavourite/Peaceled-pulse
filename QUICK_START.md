## Emergency? Do This Now

### If You're in Crisis:
1. Tap the **red phone icon** (top right)
2. Call **988** (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
3. Or text **HOME** to **741741**

### If You're Struggling:
1. Go to **Emergency Support** screen
2. Try **quick coping strategies**:
   - Take 5 deep breaths
   - Use grounding technique
   - Go for a walk
3. Complete a **daily check-in**
4. Reach out to your **support person**
